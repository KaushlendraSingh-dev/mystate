About Mainpuri

Mainpuri is a beautiful district in western Uttar Pradesh, part of the historic Braj region and near the banks of the Isan River. Known for its simplicity, culture, and rich rural traditions, it's surrounded by towns like Etawah, Firozabad, and Agra.
